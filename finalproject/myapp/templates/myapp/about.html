About Project

TOKTIK is a platform that provides services to assist you in simulating image segmentation using the K-Means dan Adapative Thresholding algorithms. As an initial identification, you can choose one of these two methods to perform image segmentation.

  • K-Means As an initial identification, you can choose one of these two methods to perform image segmentation.
  • Adapative Thresholding is an algorithm used to convert an image into a binary image.
